## 📌 Problem

Local business owners are overwhelmed with customer reviews.

* Hundreds of unanswered Google reviews
* Negative reviews hurting reputation
* Competitors responding faster
* Manual review management wasting hours weekly

Most existing tools are built for enterprise chains and cost **$299+/month** — completely unrealistic for small businesses.

---

## 💡 Solution

**ReviewReply** helps local businesses automatically manage and respond to reviews using AI.

### What it does:

- ✅ Aggregates reviews from Google & Yelp
- ✅ Generates personalized AI responses
- ✅ Lets owners approve/edit responses
- ✅ Auto-posts responses on schedule
- ✅ Tracks customer sentiment
- ✅ Sends email digests for new reviews
